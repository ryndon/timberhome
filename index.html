Our Story

Based in the heart of Southeast Idaho, Teton Timber is dedicated to preserving and sharing the art of timber frame construction. Our mission is to empower individuals with the knowledge and skills needed to build their own timber frame homes.

Through our comprehensive educational resources and hands-on training programs, we've helped hundreds of people bring their dream homes to life.
